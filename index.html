About me

I am a post-doctoral researcher at ITSA with Vadim Kaloshin since October 2024 with a main project on geodesic flows of surfaces. Beforehand I completed my Phd thesis on "Complexity and bifurcation of the identity" under the supervision of Pierre Berger at IMJ-PRG, Sorbonne Université and CNRS.

Research Interests

Dynamical systems, dimensions and scales in metric spaces, geodesic flows, renormalization of diffeomorphisms, geometric measure theory.
